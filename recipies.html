Wings, Home Fries, and Nutella Brownies

Easy Honey BBQ Wings: What You Need: - 18 chicken wings - 1 teaspoon kosher salt - 1 teaspoon garlic powder - 1⁄2 teaspoon sugar - 1⁄2 teaspoon paprika - 1⁄2 teaspoon black pepper - 1⁄4 teaspoon cayenne - Cooking spray - 1 cup barbecue sauce, preferably Hickory Smoke Flavor - 1⁄2 cup honey - 2 tablespoons ketchup - 2 tablespoons hot sauce - 4 tablespoons butter - 1⁄2 teaspoon garlic powder How to make tasty wings: Wash, dry, and season the wings with the salt, garlic, pepper, cayenne, sugar, and paprika. Preheat the oven to 375. Spray a cookie sheet with a generous spray of cooking spray. Place the wings in a single layer on the cookie sheet, and place in the oven. Cook for 35-40 minutes, depending on the size of the wings, turning once. While they are baking, make the sauce by slowly simmering the sauce ingredients over the lowest heat.When the wings are done baking, carefully dip them in the sauce, and place them back in the oven for another 5 minutes, or until the sauce is bubbling. Then serve for fun.

Homemade French Fries What You Need: - 3 to 6 of whatever potatoes you choose - 1 knife - 1 oven - 1 box of salt - 1 bottle of vegetable oil How to make awesome fries for all: Cut the potatoes into medium sized slices and lay them out on a pan. Cover in a slight layer of vegetable oil and salt. Put the fries in the oven at ____ degrees. Pull out after __ minutes and serve.

Two Step Nutella Brownies What You Need: - 4 eggs - 1 jar of Nutella - 1 mechanical whisk - 1 oven - 1 one cup measuring cup How to make these mouthwatering brownies: Crack the eggs and put them in a bowl. Now mix for 6-8 mins and then add the entire of Nutella and mix until the mix looks like a regular brownie mix. Pour into a pan with something like cookie paper underneath. Cook for 25 minutes at 350 degrees. Pull out of the oven and now you have great brownies made in 35 mins.
