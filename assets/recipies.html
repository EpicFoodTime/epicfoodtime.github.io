Easy Honey BBQ Wings:

What You Need:

- 18 chicken wings

- 1 teaspoon kosher salt

- 1 teaspoon garlic powder

- Half of a teaspoon sugar

- Half of a teaspoon paprika

- Half of a teaspoon black pepper

- One fourth teaspoon cayenne

- Cooking spray

- 1 cup barbecue sauce, preferably Hickory Smoke Flavor

- Half cup honey

- Half of a tablespoons ketchup

- 2 tablespoons hot sauce

- 4 tablespoons butter

- Half of a teaspoon garlic powder

How to make tasty wings: Wash, dry, and season the wings with the salt, garlic, pepper, cayenne, sugar, and paprika. Preheat the oven to 375. Spray a cookie sheet with a generous spray of cooking spray. Place the wings in a single layer on the cookie sheet, and place in the oven. Cook for 35-40 minutes, depending on the size of the wings, turning once. While they are baking, make the sauce by slowly simmering the sauce ingredients over the lowest heat.When the wings are done baking, carefully dip them in the sauce, and place them back in the oven for another 5 minutes, or until the sauce is bubbling. Then serve for fun.

Homemade French Fries

What You Need:

- 3 to 6 of whatever potatoes you choose

- 1 knife

- 1 oven

- 1 box of salt

- 1 bottle of vegetable oil

How to make awesome fries for all: Cut the potatoes into medium sized slices and lay them out on a pan. Cover in a slight layer of vegetable oil and salt. Put the fries in the oven at 180 degrees Farhneheit. Pull out after 90 miutes

Fun Fact: French Fries are actually from Belgium, but US Predsident Thomas Jefferson brought the recipie back from France, which is how the famous snack came to be called French Fries.
