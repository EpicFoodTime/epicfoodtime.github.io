Tofurky, Milk Free Cheese Sandwich, and Apple Juice

Tofurky and Miilk Free Cheese Sandwich

What You Need:

- 1 pakage of Tofurky from your local supermarket

- 1 package of milk free Cheese

- 1 bag of Whole Grain Bread

What you need to do: Take 1 or 2 layers of tofurky and lay it on one piece of bread. Next add 2 or 3 slices of your milk free cheese to another piece of bread. Apply the pieces of bread togther and cut into whatever shapes you feel like and serve.

Apple Juice

What You Need:

- 1 to 3 bottles of all natural orage juice

- However many cups you need

What to you need to do to serve fresh apple juice: Pour the juice into the cups, about 4/5 of the way. Serve.
